About The RoundTable

The Roundtable

Roundtable is a conference web application centered on the booking of events or conferences. The focus is on creating a platform where people and organizations all around the world can host and book events, regardless of their physical location. By providing features and tools to help enhance the experience of attendees and organizers at a conference or event we create a place where ideas really have no borders.
